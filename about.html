About Expat Explore

We are passionate about connecting people and cultures through unique travel experiences. Since 2005, we've helped thousands of members discover the world in style

Celebrating 2 decades of experience of touring our customers, we ask that you enjoy all the adventures that we have in store for you

Being a ABTA and ABTOT member, we assure our customers the best of their experiences so buckle up and enjoy our adventures knowing that you can change your minds risk free.

Our Mission

To make travel accessible, luxurious, and unforgettable for a global community of explorers. We believe in the power of travel to inspire, educate, and connect.
